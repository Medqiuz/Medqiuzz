Unit Overview

This unit covers the introduction, properties, isolation of terpenoids, overview of the isoprene rule, classification of terpenoids, and the significance of terpenoids in pharmacy. Terpenes are a large class of natural products derived from isoprene units and are found in many plants with medicinal properties.
