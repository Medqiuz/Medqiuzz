Unit Overview

This unit covers the introduction, sources for steroids, significance of steroids in pharmacy, chemistry and nomenclature of steroids, and sterols. Steroids are a class of lipids with a specific molecular structure containing four rings of carbon atoms. They play important roles as hormones, signaling molecules, and structural components of cell membranes.
