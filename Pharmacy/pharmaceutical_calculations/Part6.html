Unit Confusion

Problem: Mixing mg, g, mcg, or confusing mL with L.
Solution: Always convert to consistent units before calculating.

Incorrect Decimal Placement

Problem: Misplacing decimal points in percentage calculations.
Solution: Double-check your work and consider if the answer is reasonable.

Forgetting Dilution Factors

Problem: Not accounting for dilution steps in multi-step problems.
Solution: Track each dilution step separately.

BSA Calculation Errors

Problem: Using wrong units in BSA formula.
Solution: Always use cm for height and kg for weight in Mosteller formula.
